6. Use strong passwords

Information protected by a weak password can also be displayed in a megaphone on the metro. Obviously, it's almost impossible to remember long, unique passwords for all the services and apps you use. This is where the password Manager comes to the rescue - just remember only one combination to access it; this will save everything else for you.

  • Use long passwords — at least 12 characters, or even more.
  • Create a new, unique password for each service.
  • In order not to repeat yourself and not to forget anything, it is best to have a password Manager.
